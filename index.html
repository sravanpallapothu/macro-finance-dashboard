Financial & Macro Tracker

An open-access, auto-updating dashboard covering Indian and global financial, monetary, and macroeconomic indicators. Built as a public good — free to use, free to share.

About

This dashboard reads from a Google Sheet that is auto-populated by Google Apps Script (Yahoo Finance + FRED data, runs daily) and Python scripts on GitHub Actions (NSE FII/DII flows, runs daily; RBI Weekly Statistical Supplement scraper, runs weekly). No manual intervention needed after setup.

Four pages: Snapshot (latest scorecards by frequency — toggle Daily/Weekly/Monthly/Quarterly in the sidebar), Time Series (any indicator over any range), Deep Dive (component charts), and this About page.

Data Sources

Indicator GroupCountrySourceFrequencyLag
Sensex, Nifty 50🇮🇳Yahoo Finance (^BSESN, ^NSEI)DailySame day
USD/INR (market spot rate)🇮🇳Yahoo Finance (INR=X)DailySame day
FII / DII net cash flows🇮🇳NSE India via nsefin Python libraryDailySame day after 3:30pm IST
Gold futures, Brent crude🌐Yahoo Finance (GC=F, BZ=F)DailySame day
US 10yr yield, Fed Funds Rate🇺🇸Yahoo Finance (^TNX), FRED (DFF)DailySame day / 1-day lag
US retail gas price🇺🇸FRED (GASREGW) — EIA Monday surveyWeekly~1 day
US Fed gold stock🇺🇸FRED (WOFSRBGSA) — Fed H.4.1Weekly~1 week
RBI Forex Reserves, M3, Bank Credit🇮🇳RBI Weekly Statistical Supplement (scraped)WeeklyFriday publication
India CPI, G-Sec yield, Central Bank Rate🇮🇳FRED via OECDMonthly6–14 weeks
India Real GDP, Property Prices, Current Account🇮🇳FRED via IMF/BISQuarterly6–14 weeks

Caveats

  • Blank scorecards: A blank value means data was unavailable for that period (market holiday, exchange closed, or not yet released). The underlying data uses -999 as a sentinel which is filtered out before display.
  • Gold & Brent are futures, not spot: GC=F and BZ=F are front-month futures. Values captured at 6pm IST trigger time, not formal daily settlement.
  • FRED India series lag (6–14 weeks): All India FRED series are sourced via OECD/IMF as intermediaries. For current CPI or repo rate refer to RBI/MoSPI directly.
  • USD/INR is the market spot rate: The official RBI FBIL noon reference rate differs slightly and is not available via free API.
  • US Fed Gold Stock is statutory value: WOFSRBGSA is valued at the frozen 1973 price of $42.22/oz — not market price. US gold stock (~8,133 tonnes) rarely changes.
  • FII/DII data (nsefin) is provisional: NSE's internal API may change without notice. The most fragile part of the pipeline — monitor GitHub Actions logs if these columns stop updating.
